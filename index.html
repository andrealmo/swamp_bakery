Deep in the misty heart of the Squashy Swamp, where ancient cypress trees whisper secrets and morning fog dances between the branches, lies a hidden culinary treasure. The Swamp Bakery has been serving mystical delicacies to weary travelers and local bog dwellers for generations, transforming the wild bounty of the wetlands into extraordinary confections. Our bakers work by moonlight, infusing each creation with magic that only the swamp can provide. Open daily from dawn to dusk, 6am - 7pm, we invite you to taste the mysterious flavors that emerge from our marsh.
